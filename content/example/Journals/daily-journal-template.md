## Weekly Rollup

At the end of each week, review your daily entries and extract:

1. **Top 3 accomplishments** of the week
2. **Patterns** — what time of day am I most productive?
3. **Blockers** — recurring obstacles to address
4. **Next week's priorities** — carry forward from daily stretch goals

## Tips for Consistency

- Write for **5 minutes maximum** — brevity beats perfection
- Use **templates** (like this one) to reduce friction
- Link to relevant notes: a [[Guides/git-workflow]] or a [[Learning/neural-networks-101]] you're studying
- Review weekly, not daily — daily reviews create anxiety
